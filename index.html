Welcome to Uncle Joe's Kitchen

Lead with empathy. Show compassion. Spread happiness. We are one! Tukopamoja!

"The Secret Ingredient is always love"

Hi, My name is Josef Kariuki. Welcome to Uncle Joe’s Kitchen! We are a charity-based Youtube cooking channel; we post videos of easy-to-make, homemade recipes. These meals are prepared outdoors, in nature, and fed to orphans and the needy. While we feed dozens of orphans in each episode, ingredients for viewers who wish to simulate a meal for smaller groups of people are scaled down to correspond to serving sizes of 3 to 5.

In addition to providing quality meals for underprivileged children, we collect donations to supply them with much-needed clothes, school supplies, school fees, and shelter maintenance. I hope you subscribe to our Youtube channel and spread the word so that, together, we can lift up these beautiful children who have the potential to make the future world a more peaceful, happy place.

Join our mission to spread happiness!
